BCS English Syllabus

Course Overview

The BCS English examination is designed to test candidates' proficiency in English language and their ability to communicate effectively in a professional context. The exam is part of the Bangladesh Civil Service (BCS) examination process.

Exam Structure

Section Marks Time
Written Test 100 3 hours
Viva Voce 100 30 minutes
Total 200 3.5 hours

Written Test (100 Marks)

Components:

  1. Grammar and Usage (30 marks)
    • Parts of Speech
    • Tenses and Voice
    • Narration
    • Articles and Prepositions
    • Sentence Structure
    • Error Detection
  2. Comprehension (20 marks)
    • Reading passages
    • Multiple choice questions
    • Short answer questions
    • Summary writing
  3. Composition (30 marks)
    • Essay writing
    • Letter writing
    • Report writing
    • Paragraph writing
  4. Translation (20 marks)
    • Bangla to English
    • English to Bangla

Viva Voce (100 Marks)

Components:

  • Communication skills
  • General knowledge
  • Current affairs
  • Bangladesh affairs
  • International affairs
  • Personality assessment

Important Guidelines

  • All answers must be written in English
  • Use formal and professional language
  • Follow proper essay structure
  • Maintain word limits for each section
  • Time management is crucial
  • Practice previous year questions
