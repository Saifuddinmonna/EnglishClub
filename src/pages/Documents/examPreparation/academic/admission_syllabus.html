University Admission Test Syllabus

Course Overview

The university admission test is designed to assess candidates' academic abilities and potential for higher education. The English section tests language proficiency, critical thinking, and analytical skills.

Exam Structure

Section Marks Time
English 40 30 minutes
General Knowledge 20 15 minutes
Mathematics 40 30 minutes
Total 100 1.5 hours

English Section (40 Marks)

Components:

  1. Grammar and Usage (15 marks)
    • Parts of Speech
    • Tenses
    • Voice and Narration
    • Articles and Prepositions
    • Sentence Structure
    • Error Detection
  2. Vocabulary (10 marks)
    • Word meaning
    • Synonyms and Antonyms
    • Idioms and Phrases
    • Word formation
  3. Reading Comprehension (15 marks)
    • Passage reading
    • Multiple choice questions
    • True/False statements
    • Short answer questions

General Knowledge (20 Marks)

Topics:

  • Current affairs
  • Bangladesh affairs
  • International affairs
  • General science
  • History and geography
  • Literature and culture

Important Guidelines

  • All questions are multiple choice
  • Each correct answer carries 1 mark
  • No negative marking
  • Time management is crucial
  • Read questions carefully
  • Answer all questions
