HSC English Syllabus

Course Overview

The HSC English course is designed to develop advanced language skills and critical thinking abilities. The curriculum follows the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB) guidelines and prepares students for higher education.

Marks Distribution

Section Marks Time
Reading 50 1.5 hours
Writing 50 1.5 hours
Total 100 3 hours

Reading Section (50 Marks)

Components:

  1. Comprehension (30 marks)
    • Long passages (2-3)
    • Multiple choice questions
    • True/False statements
    • Matching information
    • Short answer questions
  2. Literature (20 marks)
    • Poetry analysis
    • Prose comprehension
    • Character analysis
    • Theme identification

Writing Section (50 Marks)

Components:

  1. Essay Writing (20 marks)
    • Argumentative essays
    • Descriptive essays
    • Narrative essays
    • Critical analysis
  2. Application Writing (10 marks)
    • Job applications
    • College applications
    • Scholarship applications
  3. Report Writing (10 marks)
    • Newspaper reports
    • Event reports
    • Survey reports
  4. Summary Writing (10 marks)
    • Article summaries
    • Passage summaries
    • Report summaries

Important Guidelines

  • All answers must be written in English
  • Use formal and academic language
  • Follow proper essay structure
  • Maintain word limits for each section
  • Use proper citation when required
  • Time management is crucial

Prescribed Books

  • English for Today (Class 11-12)
  • Selected Poems and Prose
  • Advanced Grammar in Use
  • Academic Writing Skills

Grading System

  • A+ (80% and above)
  • A (70% to 79%)
  • A- (60% to 69%)
  • B (50% to 59%)
  • C (40% to 49%)
  • D (33% to 39%)
  • F (Below 33%)
