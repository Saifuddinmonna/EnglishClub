SSC English Syllabus

Course Overview

The SSC English course is designed to develop students' language skills in reading, writing, speaking, and listening. The curriculum follows the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB) guidelines.

Marks Distribution

Section Marks Time
Reading 40 1 hour
Writing 40 1 hour
Grammar 20 30 minutes
Total 100 2.5 hours

Reading Section (40 Marks)

Topics Covered:

  • Comprehension passages (2-3 passages)
  • Multiple choice questions
  • True/False statements
  • Matching information
  • Short answer questions

Important: Passages are taken from various sources including:

  • Newspaper articles
  • Magazine features
  • Short stories
  • Informative texts

Writing Section (40 Marks)

Components:

  1. Paragraph Writing (10 marks)
    • Descriptive paragraphs
    • Narrative paragraphs
    • Argumentative paragraphs
  2. Letter Writing (10 marks)
    • Formal letters
    • Informal letters
    • Application letters
  3. Composition (20 marks)
    • Story writing
    • Essay writing
    • Dialogue writing

Grammar Section (20 Marks)

Topics:

  • Parts of Speech
  • Tenses
  • Voice Change
  • Narration
  • Articles
  • Prepositions
  • Sentence Structure
  • Punctuation

Important Guidelines

  • All answers must be written in English
  • Use clear and legible handwriting
  • Follow the word limit for each section
  • Time management is crucial
  • Read instructions carefully before answering

Recommended Books

  • English for Today (Class 9-10)
  • Grammar in Use (Intermediate)
  • Oxford Practice Grammar
  • Cambridge Grammar for Schools

Passing Criteria

  • Minimum passing marks: 33%
  • Grade A: 80% and above
  • Grade B: 70% to 79%
  • Grade C: 60% to 69%
  • Grade D: 50% to 59%
