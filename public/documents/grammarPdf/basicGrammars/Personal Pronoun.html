Details & Usages of Personal Pronoun

  1. Personal Pronoun: These pronouns are used in place of a person or individual. Personal pronouns are divided according to Person, Number, and Case. (Please read the following table carefully as it is important.) Personal pronouns can be categorized by person (1st Person, 2nd Person, 3rd Person) and number (singular, plural). See the table below for details (Kinds of personal pronoun with Number & Person).

Person Number Subjective/ Nominative form Objective form Possessive adjective Possessive pronoun Reflexive pronoun
1st person Singular I (আমি) me (আমাকে) my (আমার) mine (আমারটি) myself (নিজে)
Plural We (আমরা) us (আমাদিগকে) our (আমাদের) ours (আমাদেরটি) ourselves (আমরা নিজেরা)
2nd person Singular You/Thou (তুমি) you/thee (তোমাকে) Your/thy (তোমার) Yours/thine (তোমারটি) Yourself/thyself (তুমি নিজে)
Plural You (তোমরা) you (তোমাদেরকে) your (তোমার) yours (তোমাদেরটি) yourselves (তোমরা নিজেরা)
3rd person Singular He/ She (সে) him/her (তাকে) his/her (তার) his/hers (তারটি) himself/herself (সে নিজে)
Singular It (ইহা) it (ইহাকে) its (ইহার) its (ইহারটি) itself (ইহা নিজে)
Plural They (তারা) them (তাদেরকে) their (তাদের) theirs (তাদেরটি) themselves (তারা নিজেরা)

Table Explanation: Each cell has a relationship with all the adjacent cells. For example, consider "Me":

  • Me is the objective form of the personal pronoun.

  • Me is 1st person singular number.

  • The subjective form of Me is 'I'.

  • The possessive adjective form of Me is 'my'.

  • The plural form of Me is 'us'.
