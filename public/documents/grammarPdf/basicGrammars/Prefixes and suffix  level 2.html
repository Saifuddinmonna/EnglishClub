re-

pre-

Meaning: before

dis-

mis-

inter-

auto-

Common Suffixes for Nouns

-ness

Meaning: state, quality, or condition of

Example: Happiness (সুখ), Darkness (অন্ধকার)

-tion / -sion / -ation / -ition

Meaning: the action or result of a verb

Example: Celebration (উদযাপন), Decision (সিদ্ধান্ত), Completion (সম্পূর্ণতা), Action (কর্ম)

-ment

Meaning: action or result of a verb

Example: Agreement (চুক্তি), Development (উন্নয়ন)

-ity / -ty

Meaning: state, quality, or condition

Example: Activity (কার্যকলাপ), Reality (বাস্তবতা), Honesty (সততা)

-ance / -ence

Meaning: state or quality of

Example: Importance (গুরুত্ব), Persistence (অবিরামতা), Difference (পার্থক্য)

-ship

Meaning: state or quality, or skill

Example: Friendship (বন্ধুত্ব), Leadership (নেতৃত্ব)

-er / -or

Meaning: one who does something

Example: Teacher (শিক্ষক), Actor (অভিনেতা), Writer (লেখক)

-ist

Meaning: one who specializes in something

Example: Scientist (বিজ্ঞানী), Artist (শিল্পী)

-hood

Meaning: state, condition, or quality

Example: Childhood (শৈশব), Motherhood (মাতৃত্ব)

-cy

Meaning: state or quality

Example: Democracy (গণতন্ত্র), Accuracy (সঠিকতা)

-dom

Meaning: state or condition

Example: Freedom (স্বাধীনতা), Kingdom (রাজ্য)

-ance / -ence

Meaning: state, quality, or condition

Example: Attendance (উপস্থিতি), Independence (স্বাধীনতা)

-ism

Meaning: doctrine, belief, or practice

Example: Realism (বাস্তববাদ), Communism (কমিউনিজম)

-age

Meaning: result of an action or condition

Example: Marriage (বিবাহ), Coverage (প্রচারণা)

-al

Meaning: action or process

Example: Arrival (আগমন), Approval (অনুমোদন)

-ing

Meaning: the action or result of a verb (often used for gerunds)

Example: Painting (চিত্রাঙ্কন), Swimming (সাঁতার কাটা)

-ence / -ency

Meaning: state or quality

Example: Existence (অস্তিত্ব), Urgency (জরুরিতা)

-ure

Meaning: action or result of a verb

Example: Failure (ব্যর্থতা), Closure (বন্ধ হওয়া)

-th

Meaning: state or quality

Example: Strength (শক্তি), Depth (গভীরতা)

-y

Meaning: state, quality, or result of

Example: Jealousy (ঈর্ষা), Victory (জয়)

Common Suffixes for Adjectives

-able / -ible

Meaning: capable of, suitable for

Example: Comfortable (সুবিধাজনক), Visible (দৃশ্যমান)

-al

Meaning: relating to, characteristic of

Example: Cultural (সাংস্কৃতিক), Personal (ব্যক্তিগত)

-ful

Meaning: full of

Example: Joyful (আনন্দময়), Helpful (সহায়ক)

-ic

Meaning: having the nature of, like

Example: Artistic (শৈল্পিক), Heroic (বীরত্বপূর্ণ)

-ive

Meaning: having the nature of, tending to

Example: Creative (সৃজনশীল), Active (সক্রিয়)

-less

Meaning: without, lacking

Example: Fearless (নির্ভীক), Homeless (গৃহহীন)

-ous / -eous / -ious

Meaning: full of, characterized by

Example: Dangerous (বিপজ্জনক), Curious (কৌতূহলী), Courageous (সাহসী)

-y

Meaning: characterized by, full of

Example: Rainy (বৃষ্টিপূর্ণ), Windy (বাতাসপূর্ণ)

-en

Meaning: made of, relating to

Example: Golden (সোনালী), Wooden (কাঠের)

-ish

Meaning: having the quality of, somewhat

Example: Childish (শিশুসুলভ), Foolish (মূর্খ)

-ant / -ent

Meaning: having the quality of

Example: Important (গুরুত্বপূর্ণ), Dependent (নির্ভরশীল)

-ary

Meaning: relating to, connected with

Example: Imaginary (কল্পনাপ্রসূত), Necessary (প্রয়োজনীয়)

-ate

Meaning: having the quality of

Example: Passionate (উদ্দীপ্ত), Considerate (বিবেচনাপ্রসূত)

-esque

Meaning: in the style of, like

Example: Picturesque (দর্শনীয়), Grotesque (কিম্ভূতকিমাকার)

-ic / -ical

Meaning: pertaining to, relating to

Example: Historic (ঐতিহাসিক), Logical (যৌক্তিক)

-ly

Meaning: having the qualities of

Example: Friendly (বন্ধুসুলভ), Lovely (সুন্দর)

-some

Meaning: characterized by, causing

Example: Troublesome (বিরক্তিকর), Awesome (বিস্ময়কর)

-ed

Meaning: having the quality of (used with past participles)

Example: Excited (উত্তেজিত), Tired (ক্লান্ত)

-ing

Meaning: having the quality of (used with present participles)

Example: Interesting (রোমাঞ্চকর), Boring (বিরক্তিকর)

-ward / -wards

Meaning: in the direction of

Example: Backward (পেছনের দিকে), Upward (উপরে)

-like

Meaning: similar to, resembling

Example: Childlike (শিশুর মতো), Lifelike (জীবন্তের মতো)

These suffixes help form adjectives that describe nouns and provide more details about their characteristics, qualities, or conditions.

-ate

Meaning: to make, to act

Example: Activate (সক্রিয় করা), Differentiate (পার্থক্য করা)

-en

Meaning: to make, to cause to be

Example: Strengthen (শক্তিশালী করা), Brighten (উজ্জ্বল করা)

-ify

Meaning: to make, to cause

Example: Clarify (স্পষ্ট করা), Magnify (বড় করা)

-ize / -ise (British English)

Meaning: to make, to cause to be

Example: Modernize (আধুনিক করা), Realize (বুঝতে পারা)

-er

Meaning: to do, to act upon

Example: Remember (মনে রাখা), Recover (পুনরুদ্ধার করা)

-ed

Meaning: past action (used to form the past tense)

Example: Played (খেলেছিল), Walked (হেঁটেছিল)

-ing

Meaning: ongoing action (used to form the present participle)

Example: Playing (খেলা), Running (দৌড়ানো)

Common Prefixes for Verbs

re-

Meaning: again

Example: Rewrite (পুনরায় লেখা), Rebuild (পুনর্নির্মাণ করা)

un-

Meaning: to reverse, opposite action

Example: Uncover (আবৃত্তি অপসারণ করা), Unlock (তালা খোলা)

dis-

Meaning: opposite of, reverse

Example: Dislike (অপছন্দ করা), Disagree (অসম্মতি)

over-

Meaning: too much, beyond

Example: Overcook (অতিরিক্ত রান্না করা), Overestimate (অতিরিক্ত অনুমান করা)

under-

Meaning: not enough, below

Example: Underestimate (কম অনুমান করা), Undervalue (মূল্য কম দেখা)

pre-

Meaning: before

Example: Preheat (পূর্বে গরম করা), Preview (পূর্বদর্শন)

mis-

Meaning: wrongly, badly

Example: Misunderstand (ভুল বোঝা), Mislead (ভুল পথে চালানো)

de-

Meaning: reverse, remove

Example: Deactivate (নিষ্ক্রিয় করা), Defrost (বরফ গলানো)

en- / em-

Meaning: to cause to be, to put into

Example: Enable (সক্ষম করা), Empower (ক্ষমতা দেওয়া)

out-

Meaning: more, better

Example: Outperform (উত্তমভাবে সম্পাদন করা), Outlast (বেশি দিন টিকে থাকা)

in- / im-

Meaning: into, towards, within

Example: Insert (স্থাপন করা), Imbibe (শোষণ করা)

trans-

Meaning: across, beyond

Example: Transfer (স্থানান্তর করা), Translate (অনুবাদ করা)

co-

Meaning: together, jointly

Example: Cooperate (সহযোগিতা করা), Coordinate (সমন্বয় করা)

sub-

Meaning: under, below

Example: Submerge (ডুবানো), Subdivide (উপবিভক্ত করা)

pro-

Meaning: forward, in favor of

Example: Promote (উন্নীত করা), Proceed (আগানো)

Common Negative Prefixes

un-

Meaning: not, opposite of

Example: Unhappy (অসুখী), Unclear (অস্পষ্ট)

in- / im- / il- / ir-

Meaning: not, without

Example: Incomplete (অসম্পূর্ণ), Impossible (অসম্ভব), Illegal (অবৈধ), Irregular (অনিয়মিত)

dis-

Meaning: not, opposite of

Example: Dislike (অপছন্দ), Disapprove (অনুমোদন না করা)

non-

Meaning: not, without

Example: Nonstop (নিরবচ্ছিন্ন), Nonexistent (অস্তিত্বহীন)

a- / an-

Meaning: not, without

Example: Atypical (অস্বাভাবিক), Anarchy (অরাজকতা)

de-

Meaning: opposite of, remove, reduce

Example: Deactivate (নিষ্ক্রিয় করা), Devalue (মূল্যহ্রাস করা)

mis-

Meaning: wrongly, badly

Example: Mislead (ভুল পথে চালানো), Misinterpret (ভুল ব্যাখ্যা করা)

anti-

Meaning: against, opposite

Example: Antisocial (অসামাজিক), Antibiotic (প্রতিজীবাণু)

counter-

Meaning: against, in opposition to

Example: Counteract (প্রতিবাদ করা), Counterproductive (প্রতিক্রিয়াশীল)

Common Negative Suffixes

-less

Meaning: without, lacking

Example: Hopeless (নিরাশ), Fearless (নির্ভীক)

-ness (when used with a negative adjective)

Meaning: state or quality of being (negative)

Example: Sadness (দুঃখ), Darkness (অন্ধকার)
