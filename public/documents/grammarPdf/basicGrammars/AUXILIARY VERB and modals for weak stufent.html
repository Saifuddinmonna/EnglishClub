AUXILIARY VERB

Auxiliary Verbs Tense, Voice ও Mood এর রূপ গঠনের জন্য Principal Verb কে সহয়তা করে।

সাধারণত ২ প্রকার :

  • primary auxiliary

  • modal auxiliary

1 Primary Auxiliary /Tense Auxiliaries:

Main Verb হিসাবে Use হয় আবার সাহায্যকারী হিসাবে Use হয় (যে Verb গুলোকে বাক্যে Helping verb এবং কখনো কখনো main verb হিসেবে ব্যবহৃত হয় তাদেরকে Primary Auxiliaries/Tense Auxiliaries বলে।)

Base form V1/ present form V2/ past form V3/ past participle Ing form comments
Be Am, is, are Was ,were been being Be verb ছাড়া continuous হয় না।
Have Have ,has Had Had Having Have ছাড়া perfect হয় না।
Do Do,does Did Done Doing Do verb ,negative interrogative করতে সাহায্য করে।

2 Modal Auxiliary: ক্রিয়া সম্পাদনের ক্ষেত্রে Mood বা ধরণ বুঝানোর জন্য যে Auxiliaries ব্যবহৃত হয় সেগুলোকে Modal Auxiliaries বলে।

  • Will

  • May

  • Might

  • Can

  • Could

  • Have to/ Has to

  • Don’t/ Doesn’t have to

  • Need

  • Shall

  • Would

  • Should

  • Ought to

  • Must

  • Mustn’t

  • Modal auxiliary পরে verb এর base form হয়।

  • Be verb এর পরে verb এর ing form হয়।

  • Have verb এর পরে verb এর v3 হয়


Modals

Modal Functions Examples
Will asking (জিজ্ঞাসা) Will you go to school?
Would requesting (অনুরোধ) Would you give me a glass of water.
Shall asking (জিজ্ঞাসা) Shall I do the work?
Should suggestion (প্রস্তাব) You should work hard.
Can ability (সামর্থ্য) He can drive a car.
Could ability (সামর্থ্য) He could do the sum. Could you help me to do the sum?
May requesting (অনুরোধ) He may come here today. May I come in?
Might possibility (সম্ভাবনা) suggestion (প্রস্তাব) His statement might be true. You might go to Italy for training.
Must certainty (নিশ্চয়তা) obligation (বাধ্যতা) It must be good. You must do this.
Mustn't prohibition (নিষেধ) You must not (mustn't) run in the sun.
Need necessity (প্রয়োজন) He need not go there, [needs not নয়]
Needn't negation (না-বাচক) I need not (needn't) want a book now.
Dare bold assertion (দৃঢ়-বক্তব্য) I dare say, you are a fool, [vide - A.L.D.- Hornsby]
Daren't afraid to do (করতে ভীত হওয়া) I dare not (daren't) follow you. He dare not (daren't) do it. [dares not হয় না।]
Ought (to) moral obligation (নৈতিক বাধ্যতা) suggestion (প্রস্তাব) We ought to love our neighbors. This is really a good film; you ought to see it.
Used (to) habit (অভ্যাস) He used to walk early in the morning.
